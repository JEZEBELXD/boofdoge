Launch Plan & Token Details

Total Supply

BOOF launches with a fixed supply of 420,000,000 tokens, reflecting a playful nod to meme culture while ensuring scarcity and value potential.

Launch Mechanism

The token will launch on the Solana blockchain via a fair distribution model. Initial liquidity (32.5%) will be seeded on Raydium, with no pre-sale to ensure equal opportunity for all participants.

Liquidity Locking

136.5M BOOF (32.5%) paired with SOL will be locked in liquidity pools.

Mint Freezing

The mint authority will be revoked post-launch, freezing the supply at 420M BOOF. This ensures no additional tokens can be created, guaranteeing a fixed cap for long-term trust.
